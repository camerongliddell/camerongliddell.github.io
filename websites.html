> Pesky pigs

PeskyPigs was an idea that myself, and a few close friends had when NFTs were a trending thing! We were going to create our own NFT, and I wasted tasked with creating the website. I used multiple libraries to make sure that the website was appealing to anyone coming, as well as making a working minting button with links to Opensea where the NFTs could be purchased. However, the project fell through when people got too busy, and thus I was just left with a very good portfolio piece. The idea behind the boxes at the bottom was that the images would change every couple of seconds, being a gallery-style way to show off all of the NFTs. They also had an awesome 3D effect (Which I've also added here)!
