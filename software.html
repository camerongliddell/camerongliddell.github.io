> What have I been making in my spare time?

> Cambot

Cambot was my first big project! What once started off as a joke-style Discord bot, became a huge and serious project for me. With over 3 iterations (latest is under development with a whole new codebase), I have put plenty hours into learning and challenging myself with new technologies. The Cambot project is the main reason I have my C# knowledge to this day. On top of all of the experience I gained in this language, I also managed to experience some new and wonderful technologies such as MySQL and Linux. MySQL was used in Cambot to store player data, and I hosted Cambot from a Raspberry Pi 4 from my house!

> MobContracts

Alongside my passion for Cambot, other side of software development I came across was creating plugins for Minecraft servers. With knowledge of Java that armed me for my adventure with C#, this is where I learnt it! MobContracts was a boss-style plugin in which players would have to fight overpowered bosses for ultimate rewards! With a fully customisable configuration file to change anything and everything, and powerful code to back it up, players from all around the world enjoyed this with it gaining more than 2000 downloads globally.
